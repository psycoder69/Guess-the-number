Guess the number

About
“ Guess The Number ” is a game where you must use your logic (or simply luck) in order to guess a secret number selected randomly at the beginning of the game.
This number is guessed by you via multiple attempts.
Each time you guess, system will match your guess with the secret number & respond accordingly.
Instructions
Enter your guess & press the guess button to check if it's a match.
Use your logic to win in minimum number of guesses.
Keep an eye on the number of guesses or you might end up using all your guesses in hurry.
